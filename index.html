Selectivity

This program is built for the right founders.

Sentinel is intentionally selective. We work with a small group of serious founders each cohort to ensure real, focused attention.

This is for you if —

  • You have a startup idea you're serious about pursuing
  • You're ready to build, not just explore
  • You can commit real time — approximately 10 hours per week
  • You want structured mentorship and investor exposure
  • You're a solo founder or a team that's ready to move

This may not be the right fit if —

  • You're still in the early brainstorming phase without a clear idea
  • You're looking for a passive, low-commitment experience
  • You're not prepared to dedicate serious time to building
  • You're looking for a casual campus club or social network
